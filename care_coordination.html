Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to change my current doctors?
Not at all. We work with your existing providers. Our role is to coordinate between them, not replace them.
How often do you communicate with me?
Communication frequency depends on your needs. Active clients receive weekly updates, and we are available via WhatsApp for urgent matters.
What happens if I need emergency care?
We provide emergency navigation support, helping you access the right facility and ensuring your medical history is available to emergency providers.
Can you coordinate care for my family members?
Absolutely. We offer family health oversight, coordinating care for spouses, children, and elderly parents under one seamless plan.
