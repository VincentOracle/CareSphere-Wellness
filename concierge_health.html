Frequently Asked Questions

How is concierge different from care coordination?
Care coordination focuses on managing existing healthcare needs. Concierge adds 24/7 priority access, emergency navigation, hospital admission coordination, and comprehensive family oversight. It is our highest level of support.
Can I reach someone at night or on weekends?
Absolutely. Concierge members have 24/7 access to their health advisor. Emergencies don't follow business hours, and neither do we.
Does concierge cover my entire family?
Yes. Our family concierge plan covers spouse, children, and elderly parents under a single membership with unified coordination and quarterly reviews for all members.
Is concierge only for Nairobi residents?
While based in Nairobi, we support clients across Kenya and internationally. Our diaspora coordination services are specifically designed for families with members abroad.
