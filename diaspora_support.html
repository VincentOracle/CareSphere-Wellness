Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up my parents who live in Kenya?
Simply complete our online inquiry or WhatsApp us. We will arrange a local visit to meet your family, assess their health needs, and develop a personalized care plan. You will receive regular updates throughout the process.
How often will I receive updates about my family's health?
You will receive weekly summary updates via email or WhatsApp. For any medical appointments, lab results, or emergencies, you will be notified immediately. Monthly video calls can also be arranged.
What happens in a medical emergency?
Our emergency protocol is activated immediately. We coordinate ambulance services, hospital admission, and notify you within minutes. We also liaise with hospital staff and ensure your loved one receives timely care.
Can you help with insurance claims and hospital bills?
Yes. We assist with insurance navigation, claim submissions, and can help coordinate payment processes with hospitals on your behalf.
Do you support families in rural areas outside Nairobi?
We primarily serve Nairobi and its metropolitan area. For families in other regions, we coordinate with local healthcare providers and can arrange periodic visits. Please contact us to discuss specific locations.
