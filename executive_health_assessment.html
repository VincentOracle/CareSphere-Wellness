Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the assessment take?
The complete process takes approximately 2 to 3 hours including consultation, review, and lab work. The personalized report is delivered within 5 to 7 business days.
Do I need to be unwell to benefit?
Not at all. The assessment is designed for healthy individuals who want to stay that way. Most of our clients feel perfectly fine but want to identify hidden risks before they become problems.
Is this covered by insurance?
Many insurance plans cover preventive health assessments. We provide detailed documentation for reimbursement claims and can assist with insurance navigation.
How is this different from a regular check up?
A standard check up focuses on immediate symptoms. Our assessment includes deep risk mapping, occupational health review, stress evaluation, and a 90-day strategic roadmap for health optimization.
